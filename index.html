Hotel services include a wide range of offerings to enhance the guest experience, from basic accommodation and housekeeping to amenities like dining, Wi-Fi, concierge, and specialized services like spas and laundry. These services aim to provide comfort, convenience, and a positive stay, and can be both tangible (like in-room amenities) and intangible
1.Guest Relations: Check-in/out, luggage handling, information, problem resolution, personalized greetings.
2. Housekeeping: Room cleaning, linen changes, amenity replenishment, maintenance.
3.Food & Beverage (F&B): Room service, on-site restaurants, bars, catering.
4.Concierge: Local recommendations, tour/transport bookings, reservations.
5. Amenities: Fitness centers, pools, spas, business centers, Wi-Fi.
6.Transportation: Airport transfers, shuttle services, taxi arrangements.
7.In-Room: Laundry, minibar, special requests, maintenance.
