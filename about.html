This is my About page

Hotel services include a wide range of offerings to enhance the guest experience, from basic accommodation and housekeeping to amenities like dining, Wi-Fi, concierge, and specialized services like spas and laundry. These services aim to provide comfort, convenience, and a positive stay, and can be both tangible (like in-room amenities) and intangible

Hotel counter services, often referred to as the front desk or front office, are the primary point of contact for guests and are responsible for a wide array of guest-facing and administrative duties throughout a guest's stay, from arrival to departure. The front desk staff are the "face" of the hotel, setting the tone for the guest experience.
